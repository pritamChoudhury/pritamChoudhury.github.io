Before starting my PhD, I worked as an MPhil student in Advanced Computer Science under Prof. Andrew Pitts at the University of Cambridge. My research there focussed on the constructive formalization of nominal sets in Agda. (Dissertation)

Before I got interested in Computer Science, I studied Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee. My final year project was in detection of bad data and false data in power systems.
